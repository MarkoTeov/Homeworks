Autobiography of Marko Teov

Marko was born on 07th of June, 1993. He was born as a second child of Tatjana and Metodi in the city of Skopje, Macedonia. Currently, he is studying front-end programming as a student in the Academy for Front End Development Code Academy in Skopje, Macedonia.

In the 2012, he graduated from the highschool "Josip Broz Tito" - Skopje and later he has continued his studies in Bucharest, Romania. Very soon, he transferred to the University of Classical and Oriental Studies at the University of Sofia, Bulgaria "St. Clement of Ohrid" However, this was not the place where he has found himself, that's why he has chosen another option which was thightly connected to the IT world, so soon after he has resigned from Lycamobile Macedonia, he got employeed in Allocate Software Macedonia as a QA Engineer.

Now he is upgrading his milestones at the academy for Front - End Development
